Project Burning (March 2025)

Role: Gameplay Programming, Game Designer, Sprite Artist

  • Character movement
  • Weapon systems
  • Camera manager
  • Enemy spawner
  • Burning mode intergration
  • Weapon sprites

Project Burning is a "Boomer Shooter" that involves the player character picking up weapons as they survive against waves of enemies. It is fast and frantic, kill as many enemies as possible to get the best score! With the odds stacked against the player, thye must rely on their "Burning Mode," a form toggled that makes the player more formidable, doubling all stats while slowly sapping away their health.

On this project I created the concept of the "Burning Mode, " wishing for the player to play aggressive, with each enemy having a 50 percent chance to either drop gun ammo or health pick ups, Burning was intended to keep the game's pace quick. To this I also created weapon sprites that were to add to the feel of an older shooter.

As a programmer, I created the infrastructure for the player's controls in the style of "Halo." I also created each gun with different parameters, created the toggles between them, the enemy spawners and their spawn rates.
