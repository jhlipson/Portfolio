The Long Midnight (May 2025)

Role: Gameplay Programming & Game Designer

  • All programming and game design for "I Ate Mondays" sequence
  • Creation of boss mesh and boss textures
  • Developed enemy behavior and AI systems for "I Ate Mondays"
  • Chatter text boxes
  • Created custom audio manager for the project
  • Linking scenes between each mini-game

The Long Midnight is a dark atmospheric title that acts as 3 games in 1. Taking inspiration from "Creepshow," the Long Midnight has three smaller games each created by different Game Designers. My game, "I Ate Mondays" acts as a Garfield fan horror game where the player must collect pages much like "Slenderman" to meet their nightmare.

With complete control over "I Ate Mondays," I had say over music, sound effects, lighting, all of the mini game is scripted by me.
