The Long Midnight (May 2025)

Role: Gameplay Programming & Game Designer

  • All programming and game design for "I Ate Mondays" sequence
  • Creation of boss mesh and boss textures
  • Developed enemy behavior and AI systems for Garfunkle
  • Created audio manager for the project

The Long Midnight is a dark atmospheric prototype exploring tension, isolation, and survival in a hostile environment. Players must navigate unfamiliar spaces while managing limited resources and reacting to unpredictable threats.

The project focused on environmental storytelling, responsive player controls, and creating a sense of unease through lighting, sound, and pacing.

Systems were designed to be modular, allowing mechanics such as combat, exploration, and progression to evolve as the prototype expanded.
