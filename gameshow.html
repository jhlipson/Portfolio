Gameshow Platformer

Fast-paced platformer framed as a chaotic game show

Role

Solo Developer

Time

3 Months

Engine

Unity

Gameshow Platformer is a fast-paced experimental platformer designed around unpredictability, player pressure, and spectacle. The game presents challenges as live “events,” forcing the player to adapt quickly while systems dynamically escalate difficulty. This project focused heavily on moment-to-moment feel, camera behavior, and timing-based mechanics.
