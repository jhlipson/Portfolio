Gameshow Platformer(February 2025)

Role: Animator & Sound design

  • Created main character mesh and animations
  • Scripted animations
  • Created scripts to handle sound design

Gameshow Platformer is a fast-paced experimental platformer built around unpredictability, player pressure, and spectacle. Challenges are framed as live “events,” pushing the player to adapt quickly while systems dynamically escalate difficulty.

The project focused heavily on moment-to-moment responsiveness, camera behavior, and tightly tuned timing-based mechanics to maintain tension and rhythm throughout gameplay.

Controls

W / A / S / D
Move Character
Space
Jump & double jump
Shift
