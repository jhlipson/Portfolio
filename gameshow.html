Gameshow Platformer(February 2025)

Role: Animator & Sound design

  • Created main character mesh and animations
  • Scripted animations
  • Created scripts to handle sound design

Gameshow Platformer is a fast-paced experimental platformer built around difficult challenges, slippery controls and a rising lava that will send the player back to their last checkpoint. Collect power-ups that will allow you to dash and double jump throughout the level and used purple coloured objects to leap from object to object.

In this project I was tasked with creating the player's mesh that was supposed to mimic that of a running stick figure. I made the animations myself in Blender and scripted the character's animator controller as well as include proper Audio Source mixing for the sound design.

Controls

W / A / S / D
Move Character
Space
Jump & double jump
Shift
Dash
