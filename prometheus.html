Fires of Prometheus (February 2025)

Role: Gameplay Programming, Project Lead, Sprite artist

  • Programmed all puzzles
  • Created main scenario
  • Created Enemy designs
  • Wrote dialogue

The Fires of Prometheus is a turn-based RPG in the style of old "Final Fantasy" games. Inspired by "Paper Mario," the Fires of Prometheus is a more interactive turn-based game that allows the player to counter against incoming attacks from enemies and if countered correctly, they heal a small amount. Playing as what is called a Flamebearer, the player can either use normal attacks with interactive elements such as pressing submit multiple times to empower themselves or use magic at the expense of their health.

As a programmer I worked on all of the puzzles, framing their logic and all parts of the world around the player. Additionally, I was also the director for this small project, writing all of the dialogue and creating all of the enemy sprites other than the boss and the hooded figure named Alixia. An ambitious project, this taught me what it is to have expectations for a title.
